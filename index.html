Decisions Along The Way

Changing my major in order to graduate sooner was a decision I made because I wasn’t fully in love with being a graphic designer as a career. I felt pressured to go to college in the first place, but I feel like I would feel lost in life if I never went in the first place. I took two basic interests I had from high school: information technology and art and put them together to declare a goal of graphic design as a future for myself. It wasn’t a mistake to do that because I feel like it steered me in the direction of a future I am happy and content with. I think it’s important to mention the entire time I’ve been in college I have been working. Initially I was part-time school, part-time job, but due to the pandemic and my living situation I am noW full-time job and part-time school. Which makes the whole finishing school thing slower which can be frustrating but there’s only so much my mind can handle. Another decision I made was to work a job while in school. Its just cause my home situation calls for that. I need to depend on myself. Why part-time school though? Because 3 classes is killer already, I really couldn’t imagine taking 4 or more classes while working. If I didn’t have to work I might take 4 classes, but I can’t so it isn’t an option. My mental health comes first in this situation.
